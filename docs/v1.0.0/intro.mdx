## What is SmallPict?

SmallPict is a plugin that offloads image optimization to the cloud.

Unlike traditional plugins that use your hosting server's CPU to compress images (making your site slow while editing), SmallPict uses **AWS Lambda** to process your images instantly, securely, and cheaply.

Currently, SmallPict is built exclusively for **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**.

You connect the plugin to your own AWS account, giving you full control over your data and costs. We don't store your images on our servers; they go directly from your WordPress to your private AWS Lambda and back.
