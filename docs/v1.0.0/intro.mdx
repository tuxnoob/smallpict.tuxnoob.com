## What is SmallPict?

SmallPict is a next-generation image optimization service for WordPress.

Unlike traditional plugins that use your hosting server's CPU to compress images (making your site slow), SmallPict offloads **100% of the work** to our specialized infrastructure.

**The result?** Your site stays fast, your server stays cool, and your images look perfect.

## Core Features

We focus on three pillars:

### 1. Intelligent Conversion üîÑ
Automatically converts heavy legacy formats (PNG, JPG) into modern, ultra-light formats like **WebP** and **AVIF**. This drastically reduces file size by up to 90% without visible quality loss.

### 2. Smart Compression üìâ
Our smart engine analyzes every image to find the perfect balance between file size and visual fidelity.

### 3. Automatic Optimization ‚ö°
Just upload images to your media library as usual. SmallPict handles resizing, EXIF cleanup, and optimization in the background.
